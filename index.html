Global · Inflation + Tax Index

Where does money
die fastest?

Inflation alone is not the full story. The real pain is inflation plus tax — the double hit that hits savers simultaneously. This index shows combined damage by country.

📊 Combined Pain Index
Inflation Rate + Top Marginal Tax Rate. Shows the total annual % drag on savings. Switzerland (~13%) vs Turkey (~78%) — same $100k, wildly different outcomes.
✅ Why it matters
If you hold assets in a high-pain country, you lose more every year just standing still. Geographic arbitrage with remote work can cut your pain index dramatically.
💡 How to use this
High-ranked country: maximize tax shelters, hold hard assets, consider holding savings in lower-inflation currencies like USD or CHF.
